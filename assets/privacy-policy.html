Privacy Policy

TripKart is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

📅 Effective Date: 25 June 2026
📄

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information
When you register or use TripKart, we collect your name, email address, phone number, and profile photo.
Location Data
We collect your device location to show nearby tours, live bus tracking, and to improve route accuracy. Location is only collected when you use the app.
Device Information
We collect device identifiers, operating system version, and app usage data to improve app performance and fix bugs.
Payment Information
We do not store your card or bank details. Payments are processed securely through Razorpay. We only store transaction IDs and booking references.
Emergency Contacts
If you provide emergency contact details, they are stored securely and only used in SOS situations.
⚙️

2. How We Use Your Information

• To create and manage your account
• To process tour bookings and payments
• To provide live bus tracking and location services
• To send booking confirmations and trip notifications
• To respond to support queries and feedback
• To improve our services and app experience
• To send promotional offers (you can opt out anytime)
• To comply with legal obligations
🔗

3. Information Sharing

Tour Operators
We share your name and phone number with the tour operator for your booking. This is necessary to complete your journey.
Payment Processors
Payment details are shared with Razorpay solely for processing transactions. We do not sell your financial data.
Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government authority.
We Never Sell Your Data
We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.
🔒

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers with encryption. We use industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols to protect data in transit.
Access to personal data is restricted to authorised personnel only.
We retain your data as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion at any time.
📱

5. Permissions We Request

Location (Fine & Coarse)
Used for live bus tracking and showing nearby tours. You can disable this in device settings.
Camera & Storage
Used for uploading profile photos and travel documents. Only accessed when you choose to upload.
Notifications
Used to send booking updates, departure alerts, and trip reminders. You can manage this in device settings.
Biometrics
Optional. Used for quick and secure app login via fingerprint or Face ID.
👤

6. Your Rights

Access
You can view all data we hold about you via your profile.
Correction
You can update your personal details at any time in the app.
Deletion
You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at support@tripkart.in
Opt-Out
You can unsubscribe from marketing notifications at any time in app settings.
😊

7. Children's Privacy

TripKart is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.
📊

8. Cookies & Analytics

We use anonymised analytics to understand how users interact with the app. This data cannot identify you personally and helps us improve the user experience.
🔄

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email. Continued use of TripKart after changes means you accept the updated policy.
